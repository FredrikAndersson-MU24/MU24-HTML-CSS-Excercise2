My thoughts on reading

Since becoming a father I've read many childrens books. I believe it is a key part of a childs development in terms of their ability to learn how to read themselves and expand their vocabulary. It is also a perfect way to just spend some time together, and have a peaceful moment.
But only in the last couple of years I've discovered the pleasure of reading novels for myself, something I have not appreciated at all in the past. I now find that reading instead of swiping on my phone right before I go to bed really improves the quality of my sleep.

My favorite writers

Gillian Flynn

Born in Kansas City, MO. Flynn is known for her thriller novels.

John le Carré

Pseudonym for David John Moore Cornwell. le Carré has mainly written spy novels. His time working at the british Security Service and the Secret Intelligence Service gives his novels an added level of authenticity.

Stephen King

Crowned the "King of Horror", Stephen King is most famous for writing horror novels. Among the most well known you'll find The Shining.
