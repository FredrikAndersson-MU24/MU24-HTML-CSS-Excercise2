My favorite writers

Through my life I have not been a fan of reading books. Since becoming a father I've read many childrens books. I believe it is a key part of a childs development in terms of the ability to read and expand their vocabulary.
Only in the last couple of years I've discovered the pleasure of reading novels for myself. I now find that reading instead of swiping endlessly on my phone right before I go to bed really improves the quality of my sleep.

Gillian Flynn

Born in Kansas City, MO. Flynn is known for her thriller novels.

John le Carré

Pseudonym for David John Moore Cornwell. le Carré has mainly written spy novels. His time working at the british Security Service and the Secret Intelligence Service gives his novels an added level of authenticity.

Stephen King

Crowned the "King of Horror", Stephen King is most famous for writing horror novels. Among the most well known you'll find The Shining.
