    Introduction

    Since becoming a father I've read many childrens books. I believe it is a key part of a childs development in terms of their ability to learn how to read themselves and expand their vocabulary. It is also a perfect way to just spend some time together, and share a peaceful moment.
    But only in the last couple of years I've discovered the pleasure of reading novels for myself, something I have not appreciated at all in the past. I now find that reading instead of endlessly swiping on my phone right before I go to bed really improves the quality of my sleep. It is also a great escape from digital media for a while...

    My favorite writers

    Gillian Flynn

    Born in Kansas City, MO. Flynn is known for her thriller novels. I've only read Gone Girl, but am eager to read more of her work.

    John le Carré

    Pseudonym for David John Moore Cornwell. le Carré has mainly written spy novels. His time working at the british Security Service and the Secret Intelligence Service gives his novels an added level of authenticity.
    Read more about John le Carré (opens in a new tab).

    Sven Nordqvist

    A swedish writer and illustrator probably most known for his books about the characters Pettson and Findus. His books are loved by both young and old, not only because of his writing but also his amazing illustrations.

    Final thoughts

    With all the benefits it brings I hope I can maintain the habit of reading.
